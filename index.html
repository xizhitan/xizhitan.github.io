Xizhi Tan
CV
Hi! I'm a forth-year PhD student in the Computer Science department at Drexel University and a member of Drexel EconCS research group. My advisor is Vasilis Gkatzelis. I am interested in the intersection of Economics and Computer Science, particularly in algorithmic game theory, mechanism design and fair division. I'm a Research Intern at Google Mountain View during summer 2023.

I received my bachelor's degree from Drexel University, majoring in Mathematics. During my undergraduate years, I have worked as assistant product manager in Comcast and Digital Quality Operations Analyst in JPMorgan Chase & Co.
